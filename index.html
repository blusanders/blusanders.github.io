PROJECTS

Per Diemz - NSS First Half Capstone Project
HTML/CSS/Javascript/React/JSON

Click here to see the demo app: https://perdiemz.herokuapp.com/

The free Heroku is a little sluggish so keep that in mind :) All data is randomly generated.

Per Diems was designed to be a simple music tour management app to manage per diem and calculate bill denominations before hitting the road. Per Diem is everyone's favorite to receive. Figuring out how many 100s, 50s, 20s, 10s, 5s and 1s you need to pick up at the bank before you leave? Not so much. This app gives you time back that you can better spend on other valuable tasks.
